aboutus

Welcome to “Key Global CO-Operative Penang Berhad”, where we believe in the power of community and entreprenership. Founded on the principles of mutual support and shared success, our co-operative is dedicated to fostering the spirit of entrepreneurship and transforming lives within our community.

Our Purpose

At “Key Global CO-Operative Penang Berhad”, our primary purpose is to create entrepreneurs and drive positive change within our community. We understand that economic empowerment and business success are key drivers of community betterment. Through our various programs, resources, and support systems, we aim to provide aspiring entrepreneurs with the tools, knowledge and opportunities they need to succeed.

ourmission

Our mission is to cultivate a thriving community of entrepreneurs who can contribute to sustainable economic growth and social development. By offering training, mentorship, financial support and networking opportunities, we strive to empower individuals to take control of their economic destinies and build successful businesses that benefit themselves and the wider community.

UNLOCK YOUR POTENTIAL

ourvision

We envision a vibrant, self-sustaining community where entrepreneurship flourishes and individuals have the opportunity to achieve their full potential. Our goal is to be a catalyst for change, inspiring and enabling our members to create businesses that not only succeed but also uplift and enhance the quality of life for everyone in our community.

BUILD YOUR LEGACY NOW

Founder &CEO

Dr. KUGILAN PUSHPANATHAN Phd. h.c.

Dr.Kugilan is the visionary leader behind “Key Global CO-Operative Penang Berhad”, whereby he is driven by a steadfast commitment to empower individuals and uplift communities through entrepreneurship. Dr.Kugilan was honored “Doctorate in Entrepreneurship” by “The International University of Peace” in year 2019. Besides that, Dr.Kugilan also received a honouring award for “The Best Personal Brand Award 2020/2021”. The Bristishpedia Successful People of Malaysia Biography also has given recognition to Dr. Kugilan by interviewing and having Dr.Kugilan's biography displayed in their 2nd edition of Biography. Dr. Kugilan embarked on a transformative journey when he formed his own company in year 2015, named as “Key Global Network Sdn. Bhd.” Driven by a commitment to create meaningful change, Dr.Kugilan recognized the potential to further amplify entrepreneurship impact by transitioning into a cooperative model. Hence, with a profound passion for social impact and economic empowerment, Dr.Kugilan founded “Key Global CO-Operative Penang Berhad” with a clear mission to foster a new generation of entrepreneurs and uplift the lives of community members.

Dr. Kugilan envisoned “Key Global CO-Operative Penang Berhad” as a catalyst for change, aiming to empower individuals with the skills, resources, and opportunities needed to succeed in business. Furthermore, Dr.Kugilan spearheads initiatives that provide mentorship, training programs, and access to financing, ensuring that members receive comprehensive support throughout their entrepreneurial journey.
